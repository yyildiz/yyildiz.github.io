Space HAUC

What Is It? What Do I Do There?

Space HAUC is a fairly new (Feb. 2016) UMass Lowell sponsored program with the goal of sending a cube satellite into orbit.

My job as the lead programmer on the attitude control system (ACS) team is simply to keep our satellite from tumbling in space and to make sure that it is facing towards the sun in order to keep powering it's solar panels.
