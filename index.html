Who Am I? And Why Should You Hire Me?

I currently work as a programmer for the Space Haucs attitude control system team. Space Haucs is a UMass Lowell group with the goal of sending a cube satellite into orbit. My job as a part of the ACS team is to ensure that our satellite isn’t tumbling randomly through space, is pointed towards the sun to keep it powered through the solar panels on board, and to ensure that it actually stays in orbit and doesn’t come crashing into the earth.

I previously worked for Greenfield Savings Bank as an IT intern where my job was to handle the distribution of software to 50+ newly acquired computers and to meet with each computer recipient to ensure that they had all of the software on their new machines that they needed for work as well as, at times, teaching them how to use their new computers.

Outside of work, I enjoy programming as a hobby with Python being my favorite programming language at the moment. I also love hiking (that’s Mount Sugarloaf, Deerfield MA in the picture above and one of my favorite places to hike.), running, kayaking, and going to the gym (it’s really great for relieving stress!)
